About CountryExplorer

Our Mission

CountryExplorer is designed to provide comprehensive information about countries around the world. Our goal is to make global data accessible and easy to understand for everyone.

How It Works

Simply search for any country in the search bar on our homepage. You'll receive instant information about that country including its flag, population, capital, and more. Click "More Details" to see additional information including current weather data.

Data Sources

We use the REST Countries API for country data and OpenWeatherMap API for weather information. These reliable sources ensure you get accurate and up-to-date information.
